Research Interests

The goal of my research is to build toward a future where machine learning models can interpret biological systems with human-level intuition and mechanistic insight. I work toward this vision by developing scalable computational approaches for genetics, gene regulation, and single-cell biology. My research spans multiple fronts: large-scale analysis of single-cell transcriptomic data to derive interpretable gene programs; deep learning models for DNA and RNA sequence-to-function prediction; and methods that integrate genetic variation, regulatory signals, and ML-derived representations to uncover causal drivers of disease.
